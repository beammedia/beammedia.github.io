Comparison of Network Service Applications

Related applications and brands have been developed for domestic Internet services and applications. The usage rates for content and social media are the highest, and they are followed by e-commerce. There are fewer applications of innovative Internet concepts, and the market has yet to mature. In terms of familiarity with terms, performance in artificial intelligence has been higher.

Comparison of Current Developments in Other Countries

Comparison of Current Developments in Other Countries

The social media usage rate tops the rankings in most countries and usage on desktop computers/laptop computers remains lower than usage on mobile phones. Based on the online access behavior of mobile phones in different countries, Taiwan ranks third in terms of social media usage and the search usage rate is relatively high, trailing only South Korea and Singapore. It ranked fourth in video browsing rate, slightly behind Vietnam, Thailand, and Singapore. It ranked second with China in terms of game usage rate. Online shopping usage rate in other countries is approximately 20%.
