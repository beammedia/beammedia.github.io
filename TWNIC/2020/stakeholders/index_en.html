Organizational Performance Summary

The Stakeholders Praised the Performances of TWNIC and TWCERT/CC

The stakeholders recognized the trustworthiness and service value of TWNIC and TWCERT/CC the most. TWCERT/CC Cooperated with TWNIC to Improve the Foundation and Security Layout of Network Services

It made the ecological development of the internet society more perfect. This is a very practical combination of organizations that follows network trends.

Future Opportunities

Diverse Interactions

In addition to continuously providing services, try to replace physical events and publications with online events and publications to increase more diverse interaction opportunities under the network environment due to the impact of the epidemic.

Technical Corporations

Continuous technical corporations and technical output can also increase visibility of the brands, including educational training, technology sharing and case sharing, etc.

Development Strategy

In addition to following existing business framework to continually increase popularity, more service contents used to integrate private enterprises should also be considered.

Provide domestic information security with actual cases as reference for corporate defense and use strategic corporation to increase the visibility of enterprises.
